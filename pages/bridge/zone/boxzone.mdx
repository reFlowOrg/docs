# Zone BoxZone

Creates BoxZone

```lua
Zone.BoxZone(data)
```

- data: `table`
    - debug?: `boolean`
    - coords: `vector3`
    - size?: `vector3`
        - Default: `vector3(4, 4, 4)`
    - rotation?: `number`
    - onEnter: `function(self: CZone)`
    - onExit: `function(self: CZone)`
    - inside: `function(self: CZone)`

returns: [CZone](/bridge/zone/czone)
