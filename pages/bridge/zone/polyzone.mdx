# Zone PolyZone

Creates PolyZone

```lua
Zone.PolyZone(data)
```

- data: `table`
    - debug?: `boolean`
    - height?: `number`
        - Default: `4`
    - points: `vector3[]`
    - onEnter: `function(self: CZone)`
    - onExit: `function(self: CZone)`
    - inside: `function(self: CZone)`

returns: [CZone](/bridge/zone/czone)
