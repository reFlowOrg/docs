# Zone SphereZone

Creates SphereZone.

```lua
Zone.SphereZone(data)
```

- data: `table`
    - debug?: `boolean`
    - coords: `vector3`
    - radius?: `number`
        - Default: `2`
    - onEnter: `function(self: CZone)`
    - onExit: `function(self: CZone)`
    - inside: `function(self: CZone)`

returns: [CZone](/bridge/zone/czone)
