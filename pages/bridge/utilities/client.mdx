## GetClosestPed

```lua

```

- coords: `vector3`
- distance?: `number`

    - coords?: `vector3`

## ProgressBar

Displays a running progress bar

```lua
Framework.ProgressBar(data)
```

- data: `table`
    - duration: `number`
    - label: `string`
    - canCancel?: `boolean`
    - useWhileDead?: `boolean`
    - anim?: `table`
        - dict?: `string`
        - clip?: `string`
        - flag?: `number`
        - scenario?: `string`
    - disable?: `table`
        - move?: `boolean`
        - vehicle?: `boolean`
        - combat?: `boolean`
        - mouse?: `boolean`
    - props?: (`object` or `array`)
        - model: `string`
        - bone?: `number`
        - pos: `vector3`
        - rot: `vector3`
    - onFinish?: `function()`
    - onCancel?: `function()`
